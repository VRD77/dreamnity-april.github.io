Dreamnity Organic Content Campaign

Please review the 6 targeted UGC (User Generated Content) scripts designed for Instagram Reels & YouTube Shorts. These are optimized for high organic reach, focusing on freelancer pain points and the Dreamnity solution.

🎬 Production Setup

A-Roll (The Cast)

Cast sits casually on a sofa, leaning slightly forward. Speaking naturally but with high energy directly to the camera (shot vertically on a smartphone).

B-Roll (Visuals)

Screen recordings of the Dreamnity app, zoomed-in text overlays, or tight close-ups on props (like a coffee mug or a ₹500 note) to break up the visual pacing.

Target Runtime

Strictly ~30 seconds each. Scripts are precisely 60-75 words to ensure fast pacing and high retention rates.
