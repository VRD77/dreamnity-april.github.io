Pre-Production Document: Phase 1

Dreamnity Content Assets

Extended, story-driven 60-second scripts. These scripts utilize strong vocal hooks, relatable narrative arcs, and natural pacing to retain viewers on Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts, optimizing for organic acquisition.

Production Guidelines & Setup

Tone & Delivery

Actors will use a conversational, direct-to-camera approach. The delivery should feel native to the platform—using natural pauses and gestures rather than formal, broadcast-style reading.

Retention Tactics

The initial 3 seconds contain pattern interrupts to halt scrolling. The scripts immediately address primary pain points (GST, FX fees, delays) before introducing the platform solution.

Visual Strategy

To maintain engagement over a 60-second duration, the primary A-Roll (actor on camera) is broken up every 10-15 seconds with secondary B-Roll (screen recordings, UI elements, text overlays).
