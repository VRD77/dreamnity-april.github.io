Storytelling Architecture

The Open Loop Ending

We never ask them to follow for the next part. Instead, we end the video by introducing an even bigger problem. Example: "Getting the client is easy. But next, you have to avoid the GST trap." (Video ends). The viewer *has* to click your profile to see what the trap is.

Escalating Stakes

The problems get systematically larger. It starts with a zero-capital mindset (Reel 1), moves to looking professional (Reel 3), escalates to legal/tax rejection (Reel 4/5), and peaks with the ultimate Dreamnity fix (Reel 6).

The Grand Finale

Dreamnity is naturally introduced in the finale as the exact bridge being built to solve the painful 5-part journey they just binge-watched.
